Enchanting Grace

Roll Me

As the night falls and darkness creeps

Under the moonlit sky so clear
A sight to behold is always near
For in her smile, a glow appears
A radiance that calms my fears

Her eyes, a galaxy of stars
Shimmering bright, like they're ours
To explore and wander endlessly
Lost in their beauty, so heavenly.

I'm captivated by her grace
A presence that time cannot erase
And in the night, as we stroll
My heart feels at peace and whole.

The stars above, they twinkle and shine
A cosmic dance, so divine
But none can compare to the light
That sparkles in her eyes so bright.

And her smile, so sweet and kind
A treasure that's hard to find
It lifts me up, it brings me joy
It's a feeling that nothing can destroy.

Oh, how lovely it is to gaze
At her enchanting and wondrous face
A sight to cherish, to behold
A treasure more precious than gold.

So as the night unfolds its charms
And the moon casts its peaceful calm
I'll keep on admiring her adorable smile
And her eyes that light up my world, for a while.

For in this moment, in this night
Everything feels just right
And I am grateful for her presence
For her smile, her eyes, her essence.

So let the stars and moon above
Bear witness to this pure love
A love that's gentle, kind, and true
A love that's found in me and you.
